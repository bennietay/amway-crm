## 1. Getting Started

### Authentication
- **Login/Signup**: Use your Google account for a seamless and secure login experience.
- **First Time Setup**: Upon your first login, you'll be directed to the Dashboard. Your data is securely stored in Firebase and synced across all your devices.

---

## 2. Dashboard & Metrics

The Dashboard is your mission control. It provides a high-level overview of your business health.

- **PV Tracker**: Monitor your Personal Volume (PV) and Business Volume (BV) in real-time. Track your progress towards the next performance bonus level.
- **Quick Stats**:
    - **Total Leads**: Number of active prospects in your pipeline.
    - **Conversion Rate**: Percentage of leads successfully converted to customers or IBOs.
    - **Active Appointments**: Upcoming meetings or follow-up calls.
- **Lead Pipeline Chart**: A visual breakdown of your leads by status (New, Contacted, Follow-up, etc.).

---

## 3. Lead Management (CRM)

Manage your prospects and customers with ease.

- **Adding a Lead**: Click the "Add Lead" button. Fill in details like Name, Email, Phone, and Social Media profiles.
- **Lead Scoring**: Assign a score (1-100) based on their interest level and fit for the business.
- **Lead Status**:
    - `New`: Just added, not yet contacted.
    - `Contacted`: Initial reach-out performed.
    - `Follow-up`: Scheduled for a secondary conversation.
    - `Converted`: Successfully joined as a customer or IBO.
    - `Lost`: No longer interested.
- **Search & Filter**: Use the search bar to find leads by name or filter by status and tags.

---

## 4. AI-Powered Icebreakers

Leverage Google Gemini AI to generate personalized, high-converting messages.

- **Personalized Messages**: Open a lead's details and click "Generate Icebreaker". The AI analyzes the lead's profile to create a custom message.
- **Tone Selection**: Choose between `Friendly`, `Professional`, or `Curious` to match your lead's personality.
- **Compliance Check**: The AI ensures your messages follow Amway's professional standards and guidelines.
- **Copy & Send**: Once generated, copy the message to your clipboard with one click and send it via your preferred platform (WhatsApp, Email, LinkedIn).

---

## 5. Appointments & Follow-ups

Never miss a follow-up again.

- **Scheduling**: From the Lead Details view, click "Schedule Follow-up". Enter the date and time.
- **Calendar View**: View all your upcoming appointments in the "Appointments" tab.
- **Reminders**: The dashboard highlights any appointments scheduled for today.

---

## 6. Team Hub & Downline

Manage your organization and support your team.

- **Team Hierarchy**: Visualize your downline structure to identify key performers and areas needing support.
- **Team Stats**: Monitor the collective PV/BV of your organization.
- **Communication**: Quickly access contact details for your team members to provide coaching and support.

---

## 7. Resource Vault

Your library of sales materials and training resources.

- **Sales Scripts**: Access proven scripts for different scenarios (Product sales, Business opportunity, etc.).
- **Marketing Materials**: View and share official Amway product images and brochures.
- **Personal Notes**: Store your own successful strategies and notes for quick reference.

---

## 8. Settings & Profile

Customize your experience.

- **Profile Management**: Update your name, contact information, and business goals.
- **Theme Settings**: Toggle between Light and Dark mode (if available).
- **Account Security**: Manage your login sessions and account preferences.

---

## 9. Best Practices

1. **Daily Updates**: Log your PV and update lead statuses daily to keep your dashboard accurate.
2. **Personalize AI Output**: While the AI is powerful, always review and add your personal touch to icebreakers before sending.
3. **Consistent Follow-up**: Use the Appointments feature to ensure you follow up with every lead within 24-48 hours of initial contact.
4. **Leverage the Vault**: Regularly check the Resource Vault for new scripts and materials to keep your approach fresh.
